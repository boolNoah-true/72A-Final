The Mother We Share

The Goddess Gaia, creator of life as we know it, was betrayed by her own creations the day she introduced death to her "children". This betrayal birthed conflict between her supporters and those who oppose mortality. In response to the outbreak of war, she divided her world in twain: Earth became the refuge for those bereft of her inherited powers, while Chaos was reserved for those endowed with them. A century has elapsed since the conflict's inception, and many inhabitants have grown distant from their origins. Yet, new heroes are emerging, drawn together by the mother they share.

Chadaikumus of the Silver-Eyed Clan

Chadaikumus, the strongest aura user in all of Chaos, is the reincarnation of Nicodemus, one of Gaia's original four humans. Marked with two necklaces representing the crests of Gaia, he is known as a "Child of Gaia," chosen at birth to serve her cause.

Marisol of the Silver-Eyed Clan

Marisol, sister of Chadaikumus and twin of Rhune, is the reincarnation of Evelia, one of Gaia's original four humans. She is marked as a child of Gaia by ankle bracelets bearing the crests of Gaia. Marisol is the greatest swordswoman in all of Chaos and a psychic user. Unlike most psychic users, she lacks telekinesis and mind reading abilities but can make mental contracts with herself and others for enhanced placebo effects with real results. One of her contracts involves her and her sword, ensuring victory in almost every fight. She can only communicate telepathically with her twin brother.

Rhune of the Silver-Eyed Clan

Rhune, the strongest psychic user in all of Chaos, is the reincarnation of Issac, one of Gaia's original four humans. Marked with two rings representing the crests of Gaia, he possesses traditional psychic abilities at an extreme level. Rhune rarely speaks, as silence fortifies his mind, and he mostly communicates using telepathy.

Vuixeus of the Silver-Eyed Clan

Vuixeus, long lost brother of Chadaikumus, Marisol, and Rhune, is the reincarnation of the good in Zaius, Gaia's very first human who betrayed her. Marked as a child of Gaia with tattoos of the crests of Gaia, Vuixeus struggles with his identity as Zaius still leads the shadow users in a war to become a God. Vuixeus fights against them for Gaia and shares a strong bond with Chadaikumus.

Yuine of the Rose-Garden Clan

Yuine, Princess of Chaos and one of two blood daughters of Gaia, is unaware of her true lineage. Marked as a child of Gaia with earrings bearing the crests of Gaia, she is an aura user whose aura manifests in nature, giving her control over plants. She wields a bow with arrows made from flower stems, allowing her to guide their trajectory.

Axys of the Day-Light Clan

Axys, Yuine's sister and the other blood daughter of Gaia, is estranged from her for most of the story. Marked as a child of Gaia with pupils shaped as the crests of Gaia, visible only to those who love her, Axys is unique as both a shadow user and the first light user. Raised spiritually by Gaia, she serves as a guide for the other children of Gaia, knowing the origin of their world.

Encounter

After a tragic event, a young girl named Jikan finds herself with the power to manipulate time. This eventually leads her to encounter Supesu, her brother who, in her universe, died during birth. Shocked, she realizes they have somehow traveled between two realities, and now they must find a way to prevent their realities from merging. Jikan, with her ability to control time, and Supesu, with his ability to control space, work together to solve the universes' mysteries.

The Dance with the Devil

Seven provinces have been at war for decades. The generals of each province eventually find themselves making a deal with the devil and become hosts of the seven deadly sins. Hikari finds herself backed into a corner and close to death. During a moment of desperation, she caves in and makes a deal with the devil, but it is too late. A century later, she wakes up out of her grave with newfound powers and into a desolate world ruled by demons.
